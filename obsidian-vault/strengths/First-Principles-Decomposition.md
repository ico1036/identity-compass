# First-Principles Decomposition

Breaking complex problems down to fundamental truths and rebuilding understanding from the ground up. Core strength of [[Jiwoong Kim]].

## How It Manifests
- Deconstructing market signals to underlying mechanisms rather than accepting surface correlations
- Connecting seemingly unrelated patterns via shared first principles
- Building [[Research-Architecture-Design]] from axioms, not conventions
- Feeding into [[Dialectical-Research]] methodology (thesis from first principles → antithesis → synthesis)

## In Practice
- [[ML-Modeling]]: questioning model assumptions before trusting outputs
- [[Signal-Validation]]: asking "why does this signal work?" not just "does it backtest well?"
- [[Agentic-Hedgefund]]: designing agent architecture from research fundamentals
