# Signal Validation

Rigorous validation of trading signals through multiple lenses. Central to [[Jiwoong Kim]]'s research methodology.

## Approach
- [[Dialectical-Research]]: challenge every signal with antithesis
- [[Model-Robustness-Testing]]: stress tests across market regimes
- Kill-rules: drop signals that only work in-sample or on narrow parameter peaks
- [[Multi-Validator-Mindset]]: human + AI validators independently assess
