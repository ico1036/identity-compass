# Dialectical Research

Thesis + antithesis → synthesis methodology applied to quantitative research. Core concept in [[Jiwoong Kim]]'s [[Research-Architecture-Design]].

## Process
1. **Thesis**: formulate hypothesis from [[First-Principles-Decomposition]]
2. **Antithesis**: actively seek disconfirming evidence via [[Multi-Validator-Mindset]]
3. **Synthesis**: integrate surviving insights into robust conclusions
4. **Validation**: confirm via [[Signal-Validation]] and [[Model-Robustness-Testing]]

## Why It Matters
- Prevents confirmation bias in [[ML-Modeling]]
- Forces [[Backtesting-Framework-Design]] to include adversarial tests
- Aligns with [[Probabilistic-Decision-Making]] — no claim is binary
